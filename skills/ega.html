How to calculate Estimated Gestational Age (EGA)

Gestational age can be estimated using from the first day of the last menstrual period (LMP).

Assuming the woman‚Äôs LMP is the 20th of September, 2017 and today is the 21st of March. What is the estimated gestational age (EGA)?

The EGA is essentially the number of weeks (w)/days (d) between the two dates.

To make the calculation easier, it is important to know the number of days in each month.

note

Weeks and days in each month

  • Jan = 31 (4 w + 3d)
  • Feb = 28 (4 w + 0d)
  • Mar = 31 (4 w + 3d)
  • Apr = 30 (4 w + 2d)
  • May = 31 (4 w + 3d)
  • Jun = 30 (4 w + 2d)
  • Jul = 31 (4 w + 3d)
  • Aug = 31 (4 w + 3d)
  • Sep = 30 (4 w + 2d)
  • Oct = 31 (4 w + 3d)
  • Nov = 30 (4 w + 2d)
  • Dec = 31 (4 w + 3d)
