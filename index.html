Obstetrics
& Gynecology

Obstetrics and Gynecology (also spelled as Obstetrics and Gynaecology; abbreviated as Obs and Gyne, O & G, OB-GYN and OB/GYN) is the medical specialty that encompasses the two subspecialties of obstetrics (covering the care of women during pregnancy, childbirth, and the post-partum period) and gynecology (covering the diagnosis and treatment of the diseases of the female reproductive organs- vagina, uterus, ovaries, and breasts). It also specializes in other women's health issues, such as menopause, hormone problems, contraceptions, and infertility.
